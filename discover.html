Discover: UMD Duluth & Duluth

Why I chose UMD Duluth

I chose UMD Duluth because I had heard it was such a welcoming place with a lot to do, and because it has a new business school with strong finance programs that really fit my goals. My brother went here and several of my friends also attended, so I already felt a sense of connection to the campus before I even arrived. I liked that it offered a balance of great academics and a friendly, supportive community, which made it feel like a place where I could really grow both personally and professionally.

What I like about Duluth

What I enjoy most about Duluth is being close to my friends and having the freedom to explore everything the area has to offer. I love spending time at Lake Superior and Canal Park because they are perfect for relaxing, hanging out, and trying new things. The campus gym is also a big part of my routine, and I appreciate having access to it for staying active and de-stressing. Beyond that, Duluth has a lot of great local shops, outdoor trails, and restaurants to check out. The community is friendly, and there’s always something happening, which makes living here exciting. Overall, it feels like a place where I can focus on my studies while still enjoying all the activities and experiences the city and campus have to offer.
